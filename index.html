Our Services

Xibelani Crafting

Immerse yourself in the rich heritage of Tsonga culture with our meticulously crafted Xibelani skirts. Each piece is a celebration of tradition, blending vibrant colors and intricate designs to bring the spirit of Tsonga dance to life.

Traditional dresses

Our collection of Tsonga traditional dresses captures the essence of cultural elegance. From formal occasions to everyday wear, our dresses are designed to showcase the beauty and grace of Tsonga fashion.

Stylish Pants

Explore modern comfort infused with Tsonga style through our range of stylish pants. Whether you're looking for casual chic or traditional flair, our pants are tailored to reflect the diversity of Tsonga clothing.

Custom Creations:

At Vakahina Fashion, we offer custom creations tailored to your preferences. If you have a specific vision for your Tsonga traditional attire, our skilled artisans will work with you to bring it to life, ensuring a personalized touch in every stitch.

About Us

At Vakahina, we are more than a clothing brand — we are custodians of the vibrant Tsonga culture. Our journey began with a passion to share the beauty and diversity of Tsonga traditional clothing with the world. Every stitch tells a story, and we are honored to be the storytellers.
